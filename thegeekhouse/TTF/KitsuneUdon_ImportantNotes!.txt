This typeface requires OpenType standard ligatures to be enabled. Most programs will enable them by default, though do check if things look strange.

To make Kitsune Udon work properly in Microsoft Word, please see this page on how to enable ligatures:

	http://office.microsoft.com/en-001/mac-word-help/turn-on-ligatures-or-joined-characters-HA102928668.aspx
